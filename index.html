Wooden model Ships by Tom

My interest in model ship building started with my grandfather John Fink. He had an antique replica of the Santa Maria in his living room which fascinated me. The following is a list of the model ships I have built. The hulls are solid wood mostly Mahogany. Where the hulls are coppered this is real copper not paint. In olden days the hulls were coppered to keep off barnacles. There are no plastic parts in these models. Cannons are brass or bronze. Fittings are brass. Click on the name of the ship which will link you to photos and further information.
  1. USS Constitution Solid wood mahogany hull. Layered with copper.
  2. Santa Maria Built in 2016 recuperating from knee replacement
  3. Sea Witch Built in 1983 but completely reworked in 2018 to improve rigging authenticity
  4. Oseberg Built in 2019 from Plank on Frame construction
  5. Rattlesnake Bought second hand Model Shipways kit online
  6. HMS Bounty Bought antique but incomplete Marine Model kit online
  7. Charles W Morgan Marine Model kit built in 1979
  8. Riverboat Built in 2019 all natural finishes
  9. Gertrude Thebaud American fishing and racing schooner
  10. King of the River Originally bought for parts but decided to build it because the kit was high quality
  11. Black Pearl This is a true plank on bulkhead kit built from the keel on up
